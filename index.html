What your BMI result means

A BMI range of 18.5 to 24.9 is considered a 'healthy weight.' Maintaining a healthy weight may lower your changes of experincing health issues later on, such as obesity and type 2 diabetes. Aim for a nutritious diet with reduced fat and sugar content, incorporating ample fruits and vegetables. Additionally, strive for regular physical activity, ideally about 30 minutes daily for five days a week.

Healthy eating

Healthy eating promotes weight control, disease prevention, better digestion, immunity, mental clarity, and mood.

Regular exercise

Exercise improves fitness, aids weight control, elevates mood, and reduces disease risk, fostering wellness and longevity.

Adequate sleep

Sleep enhances mental clarity, emotional stability, and physicall wellness, promoting overall restoration and rejuvenation.
